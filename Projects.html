GeneSynth

An interactive web application designed to explore and visualize the fascinating world of genetics, making complex concepts accessible to students and educators.

Inspiration

My inspiration for GeneSynth stemmed from the growing interest in genetics and biotechnology among students and educators. I wanted to create an engaging platform that simplifies complex genetic concepts, making them accessible and interactive. By combining visual tools with educational resources, I aimed to foster a deeper understanding of how genes influence traits and health.

What it does

GeneSynth allows users to visualize DNA sequences, experiment with mutations, analyze protein impacts, and search a comprehensive trait database. Each feature is crafted to enhance learning and provide insights into genetic variations, inheritance patterns, and the biological implications of mutations.

  • Developed a user-friendly platform using React and JavaScript to simplify genetic concepts through visual tools and educational resources.
  • Implemented features for DNA sequence visualization, mutation experimentation, and protein impact analysis.
  • Created a comprehensive trait database to enhance understanding of genetic variations and inheritance patterns.
  • Integrated interactive elements to foster deeper engagement with biological implications of mutations.

BinGenius

An automated, smart waste management system that tracks waste levels and incentivizes responsible disposal behavior. Winner of the hackUNT 2024 robotics track.

  • Developed an intelligent, sensor-driven waste monitoring model using an ultrasonic sensor and ESP32 microcontroller.
  • Created a Django-based backend to aggregate data across multiple bins and provide real-time insights.
  • Implemented a rewards program to motivate consistent and responsible waste disposal among residents.
  • Designed dual interfaces: one for residents to track community waste efforts and redeem rewards, and another for waste management teams to visualize bin fill levels.
